kaomanage
How-To

use this interface to add keywords and associate kaomoji with those keywords.

then, as you surf the web, ctrl+shift+right click on any element you want to insert to display a context menu.

pick a keyword and a random kaomoji from that keyword will be appended to what you clicked on!

ctrl+shift+left-click an emoji in the kaomanage menu to delete it.
